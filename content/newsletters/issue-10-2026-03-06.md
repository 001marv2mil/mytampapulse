### Marv's Tampa Pro Tips

- **Steinbrenner Field** on Friday is going to be packed. Yankees vs Rays is the Spring Training rivalry game and tickets go fast. Show up early, grab a seat on the lawn, and enjoy cheap beer with expensive talent on the field. It's one of the best casual sports experiences in Tampa.
- **Busch Gardens Food, Wine & Garden Festival** runs weekends through May 10. The sampler pass is the move. $65 for 8 items, $75 for 12. Skip the individual purchases and just graze your way through the whole park.
- **Tampa Fine Art Show** at Cotanchobee Fort Brooke Park is free and it's legit. Juried fine art right on the Riverwalk. Saturday 10-5, Sunday 10-4. Food vendors include Just Smokin' BBQ if you need fuel.
- **Charley Crockett** at the Hard Rock on Wednesday is the sleeper pick of the week. If you're into Americana or country with soul, this dude is different. He previewed unreleased tracks from his upcoming album. $50 well spent.
- **Saturday Morning Market** in St. Pete is running every Saturday 9 AM to 2 PM at Al Lang. If you haven't been, this is one of the best farmers markets in Florida. Not an exaggeration.
- The **Crowbar** calendar this week has Currents, ERRA, Caskets, and Aviana on Tuesday. Support that venue while it's still here. Every show at Crowbar in 2026 matters.

### Hidden Gems

- 🌿 **Gizella Kopsick Palm Arboretum**, St. Pete. Over 500 palms and cycads in a quiet park with shaded gazebos and paved walkways. Free. One of those places that locals don't even know about. Perfect for a slow afternoon walk.
- 🛒 **Saturday Morning Market**, Al Lang Stadium, St. Pete. Every Saturday 9 AM to 2 PM. Consistently rated one of the best farmers markets in the state. Local produce, artisan food, live music. It's worth the drive.
- 🌊 **Corey Avenue Sunday Market**, St. Pete Beach. Every Sunday 9 AM to 1 PM. 70+ vendors. Smaller and more chill than Saturday Morning Market. Good for a Sunday cruise on the beach side.
- 🏭 **Fusillo Italian Pasta** in St. Pete. New fast-casual spot doing handmade fresh pasta. Bowls are $13-15. The kind of place that does one thing and does it really well.
- 🎵 **Crowbar**, Ybor City. I know I keep mentioning this place but it's their last year. Currents and ERRA are playing Tuesday. If you care about Tampa's music scene, show up. The venue is irreplaceable and every show counts.
