### 🎯 Marv's Tampa Pro Tips

- **BTS at Raymond James** Saturday, Sunday, Tuesday — all three nights at 8:00 PM. Tampa is the *first* stop on the entire North American leg. The whole 7-member group, all back together, 360-degree stage. The biggest pop tour in the world starts in your backyard.
- **Florence + The Machine** at Benchmark Tuesday April 28, 7:30 PM. The 'Everybody Scream Tour.' CMAT opens — if you don't know her yet, get there early. And yes, this is the same night as BTS Night 3 — Tampa's gonna be a traffic-control situation. Plan accordingly.
- **Tampa Bay Black Heritage Festival** Saturday + Sunday at Curtis Hixon Park, gates at noon. Stone City Band and Tina P on Saturday, Althea Rene and Stokley closing it out Sunday. Kids under 12 free. Easy parking if you hit the Riverwalk garages early.
- **Bowling For Soup** at Busch Gardens Friday April 24, 8:00 PM. Festival ticket gets you the show. 1,000% will play 1985.
- **The Beach Boys** at Busch Gardens Sunday April 26, 6:00 PM. Wholesome closing act for the Food and Wine Festival's final weekend. Bring sunscreen.
- **Stereophonic** at Straz Tuesday April 28 through Sunday May 3, 7:30 PM. Won five Tonys including Best Play. About a band recording an album in the 70s and watching it eat them alive. Smaller room — book early.
- **Tampa Bay Sun FC vs. Carolina Ascent** Saturday April 25, 7:30 PM at Suncoast Credit Union Field. Sydny Nasello has been carrying the attack all season — top scorer with 6 goals. Pro women's soccer downtown for the price of a movie ticket.

### 💎 Hidden Gems

- 🗝️ **Con Amor in Seminole Heights** — Oaxacan marinated steak, tetelas, grilled fish, chicken mole. Real menu, not the same Tex-Mex you've had eight times.
- 🍴 **Armature Works in Tampa Heights** — the food-hall move when you can't agree on dinner. Pizza, ramen, oysters, bao, ice cream — all under one roof, walk through and decide on the fly.
- 🎨 **Tempus Projects in Ybor City** — small contemporary art space at 1624 E 7th Ave. Four-time Best Alternative Art Space in CL. Ten quiet minutes here resets your week.
- 🌅 **Bayshore Boulevard sunrise walk** — the world's longest continuous sidewalk, 4.5 miles along the bay. Free. Best between 6:30-7:30 AM before the runners take over.
- 🏝️ **Davis Islands beach loop** — pocket beach, dog-friendly seawall, downtown skyline view. Five-minute drive from the office, feels like a different city.

### ⭐ Community Pick

> This week the spotlight goes to **Dr. Samuel Lamar Wright Sr.** — the man who built the Tampa Bay Black Heritage Festival from a single three-day event in 2001 into the largest African American cultural celebration in the region. Twenty-five years in. Started after he learned Tampa wasn't even on the map as a destination for Black travelers. So he made one.
>
> Saturday and Sunday at Curtis Hixon, when 14,000 people show up for music, food, art, and family — that's a quarter-century of one person not letting an idea go.
>
> *Wave if you see him this weekend. People who quietly build cities don't get told "thank you" enough.*
