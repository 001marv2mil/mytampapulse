### Marv's Tampa Pro Tips

- **Kunafa King Cafe** in Temple Terrace is the spot right now. Dubai-style croissants loaded with pistachio and cream. The pistachio latte is lowkey one of the best drinks in the Bay. Go before this place blows up on TikTok.
- **Brain Emoji** at Dunedin Brewery Thursday night. No cover. If you're on the north end of the Bay and want live music without spending $80, this is it.
- The **FMEA Conference** brings 10,000+ music educators to the Convention Center starting Wednesday. If you work downtown, plan your lunch accordingly. Every restaurant within walking distance is going to be packed.
- **Madame Fortune** in Ybor is a speakeasy that most people walk right past. No sign out front. Look for the fortune teller door. The cocktails are theatrical and the vibe is unmatched. Perfect date night move.
- **Pete Finds ISU** on Beach Drive in St. Pete is the new 50-seat izakaya doing Japanese-Peruvian fusion. That combination sounds wild but it works. Reservations recommended. This one's going to be hard to get into soon.
- Tuesday's cold snap is real. 37 degrees. If you moved here from up north you'll laugh. If you're Tampa born and raised you're going to act like it's the apocalypse. Either way, maybe skip the patio that night.

### Hidden Gems

- 🗝️ **Wat Mongkolratanaram Thai Temple** Sunday market. South Tampa. Authentic Thai food cooked by temple volunteers. Show up by 10 AM. Bring cash. The pad thai and mango sticky rice are the real deal. This is one of Tampa's best kept secrets and it's been here for years.
- 🗝️ **Madame Fortune** in Ybor. Speakeasy hidden behind a fortune teller door. Craft cocktails, moody lighting, feels like you stepped into another era. Don't tell too many people about this one.
- 🗝️ **Socotra Yemeni Coffee** in Temple Terrace. Small batch Yemeni coffee that hits completely different from your usual chain stuff. The owner sources beans directly and the pour-over is worth the trip.
- 🗝️ **Kunafa King Cafe** in Temple Terrace. Dubai-style croissants stuffed with cream and pistachio. This is a whole different level of pastry. Temple Terrace is quietly becoming a food destination.
- 🗝️ **Tampa Theatre** downtown. Built in 1926, twinkling star ceiling, one of the most beautiful movie theaters in America. They show classic films and indie screenings. If you haven't been, fix that this week.
