### Marv's Tampa Pro Tips

- **Tampa Bay Rodeo Bull Bash** at Hillsborough County Fairgrounds. Friday and Saturday. Justin Moore plays Friday night, Trace Adkins on Saturday. If you've never been to a Tampa rodeo you're sleeping on it. It's a whole scene.
- **Rides by the River** at Armature Works Saturday 6-9 PM. Free car show on the Riverwalk. Even if you're not a car person the vibe is worth the walk. Layer up though because it'll be in the 40s by 8 PM.
- The **Florida RV SuperShow** at the State Fairgrounds runs through Sunday. This is the largest RV show in the country and it's right here. Even if you're not buying, walking through a $500K motorhome is a free hobby.
- **Southern Culture on the Skids** at Skipper's Smokehouse Saturday night. If you know, you know. If you don't, just trust me and go. Skipper's is a Tampa institution and this is the exact kind of show that place was built for.
- **Shlump at Ritz Ybor** Friday night for the electronic crowd. If you want something completely different from the rodeo, here you go. Tampa really does have something for everyone this weekend.
- The **Village People** are playing Busch Gardens as part of the Real Music Series. Free with park admission. That's objectively hilarious and fun and you should go.

### Digest

### Hidden Gems

- 🎵 **Finest in Funk vinyl DJ set** at Flor Fina inside Hotel Haya, Ybor. Friday night. This is the kind of Ybor night that doesn't end up on anyone's Instagram but absolutely should. Great cocktails, great space, great music spun on actual vinyl. Lowkey perfect.
- 🎭 **"The Pink Unicorn"** is making its intimate theater debut this week. Small venue, powerful show. The kind of thing you go to on a whim and think about for weeks.
- 🎬 **Tampa Theatre showing "The 400 Blows."** A French New Wave classic in one of the most beautiful movie theaters in the country. Even if you don't care about the film, go for the building. You'll feel something.
- 🌱 **Plantapolooza at Sunken Gardens.** If you're a plant person this is your Super Bowl. If you're not a plant person, Sunken Gardens is still one of Tampa Bay's most underrated spots and worth the trip.
- 🎸 **Matt Pond PA at the Jack Kerouac House** (Wednesday the 15th). Intimate show at a literary landmark in College Park. If you caught this one, you already know it was special. If you missed it, put this venue on your radar for next time.

### Community Pick: Chef Suzanne "Suz" Lara of LARA

Suz Lara didn't open a restaurant in Ybor City because it was trendy. She opened it because this is where she felt it. 52 seats. A gastropub that blends Latin, Asian, and Italian in a way that sounds chaotic on paper but makes complete sense once you taste it. Korean drunk eggs. Filipino Longanisa hot dogs. The kind of menu where every item makes you go "wait, what?" and then "oh wow, yes."

People don't just eat at LARA. They text their friends about it before they've even paid the check. The buzz in Ybor is real and it's all word of mouth. No big PR rollout, no influencer dinners. Just food that makes people want to tell someone.

LARA is a 52-seat gastropub on 7th Avenue in Ybor City.

*The kind of chef who makes you proud Tampa's food scene is what it is right now.*
