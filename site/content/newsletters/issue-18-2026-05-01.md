### 🎯 Marv's Tampa Pro Tips

- **Tampa Bay Lightning Round 1 vs. Montreal Canadiens** at Benchmark International Arena. Home games this week, check the NHL bracket. The Bolts haven't been past Round 1 in three seasons and this is the most loaded roster they've had in two years. Vasilevskiy in net, Kucherov leading the league in points again. If you've ever wanted to be in the building for a playoff goal, this is the one.
- **Nelly 'Country Grammar 25th Anniversary'** at Seminole Hard Rock Tampa Event Center, Thursday April 30, 8:00 PM. Twenty-five years since the album dropped and somehow the whole thing still slaps. Hot in Herre, Ride wit Me, Dilemma. Two-hour set, full band. Don't overthink it.
- **Stereophonic** at Straz Carol Morsani Hall, runs Thursday through Sunday May 3. Won five Tonys including Best Play. About a band recording an album in the 70s and watching it slowly eat them alive. Smaller room at Straz, last weekend before it leaves Tampa, book the Sunday matinee if Saturday's gone.
- **Tampa Riverfest** at Curtis Hixon and the Hillsborough River, Saturday May 2. Free entry, free water ski show on the river, free music with Sixpence None The Richer headlining the main stage. 18-year tradition, brings 30,000 people downtown. Park at Poe Garage early, walk the Riverwalk in.
- **Bleed From Within** at Orpheum Theatre, Friday May 1, 6:00 PM. Scottish metalcore that actually melts faces. The Orpheum is a small Ybor room and these guys play big, bring earplugs and get there for openers.
- **The Queers** at The Brass Mug, Friday May 1, 6:30 PM. $25. Pop punk lifers, 40 years deep, three chords and a mean streak. The Mug is the right room for this — sticky floors, $4 PBR, exactly the vibe.
- **Tampa Tarpons home stand vs. St. Lucie Mets** at George Steinbrenner Field, Tuesday through Sunday. Yankees High-A affiliate, $10 tickets, $5 hot dogs, real ballpark with zero of the Trop drive. Saturday night is fireworks night, bring the kids.

### 📋 Digest

- 🔥 **Lightning Round 1 swings home this week** — the Canadiens series rolls into Benchmark and Channelside is gearing up for a full takeover. Expect packed bars, Bolts jerseys everywhere, and a city that lives and dies on every shot.
- 🏟️ **Stereophonic wraps its Tampa run Sunday** — five Tonys including Best Play, last chance to catch it at Straz before the touring production rolls out. The Sunday matinee usually has the best seats left.
- 🎵 **Tampa Riverfest returns Saturday** — Sixpence None The Richer headlines, plus the annual water ski show on the Hillsborough River, food trucks lining Curtis Hixon, all free. 18-year tradition.
- 🎨 **Cinco de Dodgeball lands Saturday** — annual benefit dodgeball tournament that kicks Cinco weekend off a day early. Teams of six, costumes encouraged, beer included.
- 🌟 **Romance Lit Fest takes over the Bay this weekend** — Steamy Lit Bookstore plus a packed author lineup hosting events at multiple Bay venues. If you like romance novels, this is your Coachella.
- 📍 **Hikari food hall on N Florida Ave is getting closer** — 8-9 concepts under one roof from Champion Pizza's Hakki Akdeniz. Watch the Seminole Heights corner, the food density there keeps climbing.
- 🎉 **Ybor Saturday Market at Centennial Park** — every Saturday, oldest continuously running outdoor market in the Bay. Local artists, gourmet snacks, dogs welcome.

### 💎 Hidden Gems

- 🗝️ **West Tampa Sandwich Shop on W Columbus Dr** — the Cuban Tampa natives swear by, $8, pressed plain, nothing fancy. Cash works, line moves fast, lunch only.
- 🍳 **Tarpon Springs sponge docks** — real Greek food, real working boats, zero kitsch once you walk two streets back from the water. Order the spanakopita at Hellas, walk the docks, drive home through Dunedin.
- 🛍️ **Datz Dough on S Howard** — donut counter most South Tampa locals forget exists. Maple bacon long john before 10AM, pair it with the cold brew. Saturday morning ritual.
- 🌅 **Davis Islands beach loop** — pocket beach, dog-friendly seawall, downtown skyline view across the water. Five-minute drive from downtown, feels like a different city.
- 🎨 **Tempus Projects in Ybor City** — small contemporary art space at 1624 E 7th Ave. Four-time Best Alternative Art Space in Creative Loafing. Ten quiet minutes here resets your week.

### ⭐ Community Pick

> This week the spotlight goes to **the volunteers running Tampa Riverfest**. The thing has been free for nearly two decades. Free music, free water ski show, free food trucks, free everything. None of that happens without 100+ people showing up at 6AM Saturday to set up tents, run sound, hand out water, and then quietly take the whole thing down at midnight while the rest of us drive home full and happy.
>
> Saturday at Curtis Hixon, when 30,000 people walk in and don't pay a dime, that's not magic. That's a small army of locals deciding their city deserves a free festival.
>
> *Wave if you see them this weekend. The people who quietly make a city feel like a city don't get told "thank you" enough.*
