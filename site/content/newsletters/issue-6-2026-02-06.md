### 🎯 Marv's Tampa Pro Tips

- **Armature Works** is doing a free Super Bowl watch party with a giant outdoor screen. Get there early because this place fills up fast and the Riverwalk crowd spills in. Bring a chair if you can.
- **RumFish Grill** is running all-you-can-eat wings for the game. That's not a drill. If you're serious about Super Bowl food, this is the play.
- **Asian Food Festival at Yuengling Kitchen** is combining Lunar New Year and Super Bowl into one event. That's the kind of crossover episode Tampa does better than anywhere else.
- **Con Amor** opens tonight in Seminole Heights and they're doing house-nixtamalized masa. If you don't know what that means, just know it's the difference between good Mexican food and life-changing Mexican food. Go early. First week crowds are real.
- **Florida State Fair** pro move: go on a weekday if you can. Weekend lines for rides are brutal. Tuesday or Wednesday you'll walk onto everything. Also the drone show at night is genuinely impressive. Don't leave before dark.
- **The Choir of Man** at Straz has two shows Saturday (6 PM and 9 PM). It's a musical set in a pub where they actually serve you drinks during the show. The 9 PM show has a completely different energy than the 6. Go late.

### 💎 Hidden Gems

- 🛶 **Lettuce Lake Regional Park.** 17 miles northeast of downtown. Kayak through some of the most scenic wetlands in Florida. Boardwalk trails, cypress canopies, and the kind of quiet that makes you forget you live in a city. This is one of Tampa's best kept secrets and it's been here the whole time.
- 🛒 **Fresh Market at Wiregrass.** 50+ vendors. Produce, baked goods, artisan everything. If you've been doing the same grocery store loop for months, break the cycle and go wander through this on a Saturday morning. You'll leave with stuff you didn't know you needed.
- 🐕 **Two Shepherds Taproom.** Off-leash dog park plus a bar with 20 craft beers on tap. That's it. That's the pitch. If you have a dog and you like beer, why haven't you been here yet. Seriously.
- 🎬 **Tampa Theatre Black Love Classics series.** Running all February. They're screening classic films celebrating Black love and each one feels like an event. The theater alone is worth showing up for. This week it's "The Photograph" on Saturday.

### 💘 Valentine's Day Preview

Valentine's Day is next Saturday and if you haven't planned something yet, you're running out of time. Here's your cheat sheet:

- **Sant' Yago Knight Parade**, Feb 14 in Ybor City. 100+ organizations marching through the streets. 35,000 people expected. Grand Marshal is Carlos Fuente Jr. of Arturo Fuente Cigars fame. This is a massive, free, uniquely Tampa night out.
- **Lunar Lantern Festival**, Feb 14-15 in Dade City. Thousands of sky lanterns being released at once. 75+ Asian food vendors. If you want something genuinely magical and different, this is it. Photos from this event are unreal.
- **& Juliet matinee**, Feb 14 at Straz Center. A pop musical for Valentine's Day afternoon. Smart move if dinner is your evening plan.
- **MOSI Science After Dark** tonight is love-themed if you want to get the Valentine's energy started early.
