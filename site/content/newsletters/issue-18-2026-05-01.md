### 💎 Hidden Gems

- 🗝️ **West Tampa Sandwich Shop** on W Columbus Dr — the Cuban Tampa natives swear by. Order it pressed and plain. No fancy add-ons.
- 🍳 **Tarpon Springs sponge docks** — real Greek food, working boats, zero kitsch once you walk two streets back from the water. Worth the 45-minute drive.
- 🛍️ **Datz Dough on S Howard** — donut counter most South Tampa locals forget exists. Maple bacon long john before 10AM, pair it with the cold brew.
- 🌅 **Davis Islands beach loop** — pocket beach, dog-friendly seawall, downtown skyline view. Five-minute drive from downtown, feels like a different city.
- 🎨 **Tempus Projects in Ybor City** — small contemporary art space at 1624 E 7th Ave. Four-time Best Alternative Art Space in CL. Ten quiet minutes here resets your week.

### ⭐ Community Pick

> This week the spotlight goes to **the volunteers running Tampa Riverfest**. The thing has been free for almost two decades. Free music, free water ski show, free food trucks, free everything. None of that happens without 100+ people showing up at 6AM Saturday to set up tents and pulling them back down at midnight.
>
> *Wave if you see them this weekend. The people who quietly make a city feel like a city don't get told "thank you" enough.*
